Five routes through the same deep sea

The body does not start as a class. Organs, synergies, and damage risks push a run toward different rhythms, from clean kiting to risky close-range pressure.

Kite

Remote venom and spacing

Keep distance, mark targets, stack poison, and let ranged organs keep working while the swarm chases through bad angles.

Fits players who like movement, retreat lines, and damage over time.
Melee

Heavy armor and regeneration

Stack shells, regeneration tissue, and close-range organs, then pressure enemies at the edge of what the body can repair.

The mantis shrimp cut leans into this route, with ranged organs covering unsafe gaps.
Field

Mucus, electric grids, and zones

Lay down fluid, toxin, or electric areas, then pull enemies across the edge where control and damage overlap.

A steadier route for players who like shaping the arena before the harvest.
Assassin

Camouflage and first strike

Use disguise to choose the target, open with a burst window, then leave before the body is forced into a fair fight.

Strong timing can pay off, but a missed window should feel dangerous.
Support

Symbiosis and support net

Use lures, cleaner shrimp, and symbiotic areas as a support layer while the player still makes the main combat decisions.

Support helps stabilize a run, but marked threats still need attention.

Build a body that can fail

Hetero-Genesis is about the run you can hold together, not a fixed class or weapon list. The deeper you go, the more each body choice starts to matter.

1

深海 Roguelite 深潜

Pick a species, enter a short pressure-heavy dive, survive waves, draft rewards, and push toward the next boss.

2

器官装配与身体槽位

Shells, venom glands, tentacles, rams, electric organs, sonic organs, and regeneration tissues all need room on the body.

3

协同效果

Organs can unlock combat rules together, from poison pressure and electric control to defense, recovery, summons, and burst windows.

4

器官受损、断裂、修复

Important organs can be damaged or broken. A strong build can lose a key link until you find a repair window.

5

Boss 后演化三选

After boss fights, choose between deepening instinct, grafting ecology, or unstable mutation to steer the next stretch.

6

深潜契约与 A 难度

Abyss difficulty and dive contracts let later runs add pressure without changing the heart of the game.

7

局外成长

Gene points unlock more species, contracts, and long-term options between dives.
